This is my Portfolio. I make games on Construct 3. I love game design, especially cute and/or chaotic games, but I also like horror and fantasy.

ABOUT ME

I'm 23 years old and I just finished college (Game Development). I like planning games and making them on Construct 3. I love doing game jams especially 'cause I think I work better if I have a short deadline.
Apart from game design and game jams, I love voice acting and I do some freelance voice acting work. I've been practing it since 2014 and I learned audio editing because of it.
Since I know audio editing I always care about that a lot when I make a game. I usually edit using Sony Vegas Pro 14.
I also learned Photoshop so I could edit thumbnail images and because of that I started making some better art for my games. I don't make all the art but I make all the text and buttons, sometimes the background and some assets.
My biggest plan is to move to Canada. I have English C2 level (CERTIFICATE HERE). I want to work with game design and also I will try to be a voice actor in Canada too, and I could be one in Brazil at the same time since now many studios are recording remotely.

TL;DR: I like making games, voice acting, audio editing and editing in Photoshop.
